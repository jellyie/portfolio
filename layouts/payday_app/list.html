The App Redesigned

When redesigning the app, my main focus was on simplifying the app from a user's perspective. The original application was too time consuming to use, and I hope by simplyifing the app, I would also be reducing the resistance to using it.

Previous Iteration
  • Takes up users' precious time
  • Unintuitive and difficult to use
  • Only tracks salary and hours worked
Solutions and New Features
  • Easy QR clocking in system
  • Unified system to contact coworkers and receive work announcements
  • Easy to flag shifts, request days off, look for shift covers or swap shifts
  • Detailed salary tracking page with charts and stats
  • Access to detailed information regarding shifts: Who's working? Particular notices for the day?
